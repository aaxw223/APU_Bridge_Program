Culture through Creativity

Art Bridges is a 3D virtual gallery celebrating cultural diversity and creativity. Explore stunning artwork from around the world, including highlights from Japan,Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Myanmar. With interactive navigation and immersive design, it’s an engaging way to experience global artistry from anywhere.

Instructions:

  • left-click: Enter the scene
  • ESC: Unlock the mouse
  • WASD: Move around:
    • W: Move forward
    • A: Move left
    • S: Move backward
    • D: Move right
  • Mouse: Look around

    • For Smart Phones: use Move Option to Move And Touch to look
